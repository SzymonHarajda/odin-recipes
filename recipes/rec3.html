Whole Wheat Pancake Mix

This is a bulk whole wheat pancake mix recipe that my family loves, and it is very easy to make! It also makes good waffles.

Ingredients

  1. 5 cups whole wheat flour
  2. 3 cups unbleached all-purpose flour
  3. ½ cup white sugar
  4. 2 ½ tablespoons baking powder
  5. 4 teaspoons baking soda
  6. 4 teaspoons salt

Directions

    Step 1

  • Whisk whole wheat flour, all-purpose flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt together in a bowl; store in an airtight container.
